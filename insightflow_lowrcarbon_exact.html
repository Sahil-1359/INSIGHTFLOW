FAQ

Frequently asked questions

In case you had questions before uploading your first file.

Does my data ever leave my browser? +
No. All CSV parsing, statistical computation, and metric extraction happens entirely in your browser using PapaParse. Your raw data rows never touch a server. Only the summarised analysis results (not the raw data) are sent to GPT-4 for insight synthesis.
What does 3σ anomaly detection actually do? +
For each numeric column, InsightFlow calculates the mean and standard deviation. Any value more than 3 standard deviations from the mean is flagged as an outlier. This catches genuine business spikes as well as data-entry errors that would skew your analysis.
Which file types are supported? +
Currently .csv files only. The parser handles comma, semicolon, and tab-separated formats automatically. Max file size is 50MB. Support for Excel (.xlsx) is on the roadmap.
How does the GPT-4 integration work? +
After local analysis completes, InsightFlow sends a structured summary of the findings — column names, detected metrics, outlier rates, trend direction — to the GPT-4 API. The raw row data is never sent. GPT-4 then generates plain-language recommendations based on the summary.
Can I compare multiple LLMs? +
Yes — on the Pro plan, InsightFlow benchmarks GPT-4, Claude, and Gemini side-by-side on the same analysis summary, so you can compare the quality and specificity of each model's recommendations directly.
Is there a free plan? +
Yes. The free plan lets you upload up to 3 CSVs per day and access the full dashboard, completeness scoring, and anomaly detection — no credit card required. GPT-4 insight synthesis is a Pro feature.
